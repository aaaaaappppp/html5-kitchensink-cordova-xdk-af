This is panel.html

This was loaded via AJAX by setting attribute data-persist-ajax="true" and setting the href="panel.html" in the link. The content is inserted into it's own div and it now exists as a panel.

Setting data-refresh-ajax="true" will force a refresh of the content for the div based off the link. The panel is still kept in the DOM, but the content is updated.

Setting title attribute in the link will make it the panel header title.
